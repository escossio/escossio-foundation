# Experience Package

## Definition

Experience Package is the official concept used by the Escossio Foundation to describe a complete experience ready for consumption by another platform.

It does not describe implementation.
It describes distribution of experience across the ecosystem.

## Scope

The concept belongs to the Escossio ecosystem, not to a single product.

It was promoted from the evolution of Agenda Falante into an official foundation concept.

## Properties

- represents a complete experience;
- carries intent rather than platform logic;
- keeps consumers decoupled from internal engines;
- can be reused across platforms;
- supports future evolution without changing the conceptual core.

## Relationship to the Foundation

Experience Package is now part of the central conceptual vocabulary of the Escossio Foundation.

It connects identity, context, narrative, and delivery without exposing internal mechanics.
