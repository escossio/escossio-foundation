# Design Language

## Purpose

The Escossio ecosystem should present a design language that is clear, natural, low-friction, and respectful of context.

## Qualities

- Clarity.
- Naturalness.
- Low friction.
- Respect for context.
- Transparency.
- A sense of intelligence without being invasive.
- Technology that disappears so the experience can appear.

## Direction

Products in the ecosystem should feel calm, deliberate, and useful.

The user should perceive intention, not machinery.
